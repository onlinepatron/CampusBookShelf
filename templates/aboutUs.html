About Us

The CampusBookShelf team believes everyone deserves access to books, and with this platform we hope to improve the issue of overpriced and inaccessible books. We are CITS3403 Agile Web Development students who have a passion for the subject, using our skills and knowledge to create a platform for the better good. As avid book readers ourselves we hope to share this passion and knowledge with all students by sharing and extending our bookshelf. Join our mission to make books more accessible and enjoyable for all by contributing to the Campus Bookshelf!
